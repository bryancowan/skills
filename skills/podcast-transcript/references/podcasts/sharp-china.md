# Podcast Profile: Sharp China

A worked example of a podcast profile. Copy this file to
`references/podcasts/<your-slug>.md` to add your own podcast. The skill loads a
profile to pre-fill speakers and known speech-to-text artifacts; without one it
infers everything from the transcript.

## Speakers

- **Andrew Sharp** — host/interviewer: introduces segments, asks questions, reads
  listener emails and quoted material, makes transitions between topics, short
  affirmations ("Right.", "Yeah.", "Interesting."), addresses Bill by name
  ("Bill, what do you think of…").
- **Bill Bishop** — analyst/guest: long-form China analysis, references his
  Sinocism newsletter, shares specific facts and events, opens sentences with
  "I mean," / "I think," / "You know,".
